Prophet Salih

The She-Camel Sign

☀️🪨🐪🌴

🏜️ A Strong People

The people of Thamud carved homes into mountains and thought they were very powerful. Prophet Salih reminded them that every blessing comes from Allah.

He asked them to worship Allah alone, stop arrogance, and live with justice.

هَٰذِهِ نَاقَةُ اللَّهِ لَكُمْ آيَةً

"This is the she-camel of Allah, a sign for you."

— Surah Al-A'raf (7:73)

🐪 A Clear Sign

Allah gave them a special sign: a she-camel. Salih told his people to let her drink and not harm her.

Some people still refused to obey. The story reminds us that signs from Allah are not games. They must be respected.

🌟 What We Learn from Salih's Story

🐪

Respect Allah's signs. Do not mock what Allah has made important.

🤝

Be fair to people and animals. Islam teaches mercy and justice.

🌱

Power should make us grateful. Strength is a test from Allah.
