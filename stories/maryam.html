Maryam

Worship, Purity, and a Great Miracle

✨ ⭐ 🕌 🌴 🌸 🕊️

🕌 A Life of Worship

Maryam was one of the most honoured women in the Quran. From a young age, she loved worship, purity, and remembering Allah.

She spent time in devotion and trusted Allah deeply. Whenever her guardian Zakariyya visited her, he found blessings with her and was amazed by Allah's care for her.

🌟 Glad Tidings from Allah

Allah sent an angel to Maryam with astonishing news: she would have a son by Allah's command. Maryam was surprised, but she submitted to Allah's will.

This son would be Prophet Isa, a noble messenger. The miracle showed that Allah creates however He wills.

إِنَّ اللَّهَ اصْطَفَاكِ وَطَهَّرَكِ وَاصْطَفَاكِ عَلَىٰ نِسَاءِ الْعَالَمِينَ

"Indeed, Allah has chosen you, purified you, and chosen you above the women of the worlds."

— Surah Aal 'Imran (3:42)

🌴 Strength in a Difficult Moment

During a difficult time, Allah comforted Maryam near a date palm. She was given fresh dates and flowing water and was told not to lose heart.

Allah protected her and honoured her. Maryam's story is full of dignity, trust, and quiet strength.

🌟 What We Learn from Maryam's Story

🕌

Worship shapes the heart. Maryam stayed close to Allah from a young age.

🌸

Purity and dignity matter. Maryam is a model of sincerity and honour.

💧

Allah gives comfort in hard moments. He cared for Maryam when she needed strength.

✨

Allah's plan is perfect. Even surprising events happen by His wisdom and mercy.
