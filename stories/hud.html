Prophet Hud

Strength Belongs to Allah

☁️☁️🌪️🏛️

🏛️ The People of Aad

The people of Aad were tall, strong, and proud of their buildings. They forgot that strength is a gift from Allah.

Prophet Hud called them gently to worship Allah alone and stop being arrogant.

فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ

"So fear Allah and obey me."

— Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:131)

🌪️ A Warning Ignored

Most of the people refused and mocked Hud. They trusted their muscles and buildings more than they trusted the truth.

Allah saved Hud and the believers, and the proud people learned that no one is stronger than Allah.

🌟 What We Learn from Hud's Story

🤲

Be humble. Every strength and blessing comes from Allah.

🧭

Listen to good advice. Warnings can be mercy.

🌤️

Allah protects believers. Trust Him in hard times.
