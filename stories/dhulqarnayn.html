Dhul-Qarnayn

A Just Journey Across the Earth

⛰️🏔️🧱🧭

🧭 A Leader with Justice

Allah gave Dhul-Qarnayn power and ability, but he did not use it to be cruel. He traveled with justice and helped people.

Wherever he went, he remembered that Allah was watching and that leadership is a responsibility.

هَٰذَا رَحْمَةٌ مِّن رَّبِّي

"This is a mercy from my Lord."

— Surah Al-Kahf (18:98)

🧱 Building Together

A group of people asked him for help against Ya'juj and Ma'juj. Instead of taking their money, he asked them to work with him.

Together they built a strong barrier. When it was finished, Dhul-Qarnayn did not boast. He said it was mercy from Allah.

🌟 What We Learn from Dhul-Qarnayn's Story

🤝

Help people. Strength is best when it protects others.

🧱

Teamwork matters. Big problems need patient work together.

🤲

Thank Allah. Never take full credit for blessings.
