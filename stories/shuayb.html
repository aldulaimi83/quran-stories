Prophet Shuayb

Honesty in Every Deal

⚖️🏘️🪙💧

🛒 A Marketplace Problem

Some people in Shuayb's town cheated in buying and selling. They took more for themselves and gave others less than they deserved.

Prophet Shuayb reminded them that Allah sees every scale, every coin, and every promise.

أَوْفُوا الْكَيْلَ وَلَا تَكُونُوا مِنَ الْمُخْسِرِينَ

"Give full measure and do not be of those who cause loss."

— Surah Ash-Shu'ara (26:181)

⚖️ Fairness is Worship

Shuayb taught that being honest is part of worship. A good Muslim is truthful at home, at school, and in business.

He wanted his people to become clean-hearted and fair, but many refused his advice.

🌟 What We Learn from Shuayb's Story

✅

Never cheat. Allah loves honesty.

⚖️

Give people their rights. Fairness makes communities safe.

👀

Allah sees everything. Even small choices matter.
