Luqman the Wise

Gentle Advice for a Beautiful Heart

📖💛🧒🪔

💛 A Wise Father

Luqman was known for wisdom. He gave his son advice with love, not anger, because good advice is easier to accept when it is gentle.

His first advice was the most important: worship Allah alone and never place anything beside Him.

يَا بُنَيَّ لَا تُشْرِكْ بِاللَّهِ

"O my son, do not associate anything with Allah."

— Surah Luqman (31:13)

🌱 Advice for Life

Luqman taught his son to pray, be patient, respect parents, avoid arrogance, walk humbly, and speak with a calm voice.

These lessons are not only for one child. They are gifts for every family.

🌟 What We Learn from Luqman's Story

☝️

Worship Allah alone. Tawheed is the first lesson.

👨‍👩‍👧

Honor your parents. Gratitude begins at home.

🤫

Speak gently. A calm voice is beautiful.
