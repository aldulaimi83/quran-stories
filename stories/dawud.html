Prophet Dawud

A Brave Prophet with a Beautiful Voice

☀️⛰️🐦🕊️📖

🛡️ Courage with Faith

Dawud was young, but his heart was strong because he trusted Allah. When others were afraid, Dawud remembered that victory comes from Allah, not from size or weapons.

Allah gave Dawud wisdom, courage, and a beautiful voice. He became a prophet and a just king who cared about his people.

وَآتَيْنَا دَاوُودَ زَبُورًا

"And We gave Dawud the Zabur."

— Surah An-Nisa (4:163)

🎵 A Voice that Praised Allah

Dawud loved remembering Allah. When he recited and praised Allah, even the mountains and birds joined him by Allah's command.

His beautiful voice was not for showing off. It was a gift used to worship Allah and soften hearts.

🌟 What We Learn from Dawud's Story

💪

True courage comes from Allah. A faithful heart can be stronger than fear.

⚖️

Use power with justice. Dawud ruled with wisdom and fairness.

🎼

Use your gifts for good. Dawud used his voice to praise Allah.
