Prophet Ayub

A Story of Patience and Trust

☁️ ☁️ 🏡 🌳 🤍 💦

🌿 Blessed and Thankful

Prophet Ayub was known for his worship, kindness, and gratitude. Allah blessed him with family, health, and wealth, and Ayub always thanked Allah for every blessing.

But the real beauty of Ayub's story came when his life changed. Hard times arrived, one after another, and Ayub lost much of what he had.

⏳ Hardship with Patience

Ayub became ill and weak for a long time. Many people left him, but he never blamed Allah and never stopped making du'a. He stayed patient, gentle, and faithful.

He prayed with respect and humility, "Indeed, hardship has touched me, and You are the Most Merciful of those who show mercy."

أَنِّي مَسَّنِيَ الضُّرُّ وَأَنتَ أَرْحَمُ الرَّاحِمِينَ

"Indeed, hardship has touched me, and You are the Most Merciful of the merciful."

— Surah Al-Anbiya (21:83)

💦 Relief from Allah

Allah answered Ayub's prayer. He was told to strike the ground, and a cool spring appeared. Ayub washed and drank from it, and Allah restored his health.

Allah also returned blessings to him and rewarded his patience. Ayub's story reminds us that even very long hardship can end with Allah's mercy.

🌟 What We Learn from Ayub's Story

⏳

Patience is powerful. Ayub stayed patient for a long time without losing hope.

🤲

Make du'a in hardship. Ayub turned to Allah with beautiful words.

💚

Allah's mercy is greater than our pain. Relief can come after even the hardest days.

🌤️

Stay grateful in every season. Ayub thanked Allah in ease and in hardship.
