Prophet Isa

A Messenger of Mercy and Truth

☀️ 🕊️ 🕊️ 🌴 🕯️ 🌼

🌟 A Miraculous Birth

Allah chose Maryam for a very special honour. By Allah's command, she gave birth to Prophet Isa without a father. This was one of the great miracles of Allah.

When people were shocked and confused, Allah made baby Isa speak from the cradle. He declared that he was a servant of Allah and a prophet.

إِنِّي عَبْدُ اللَّهِ آتَانِيَ الْكِتَابَ وَجَعَلَنِي نَبِيًّا

"Indeed, I am the servant of Allah. He has given me the Scripture and made me a prophet."

— Surah Maryam (19:30)

🕊️ A Message of Mercy

As he grew, Isa called people to worship Allah alone and to live with sincerity, kindness, and obedience. He spoke with wisdom and reminded people to soften their hearts.

Allah gave Isa miracles by His permission. He healed the sick, helped the blind, and brought comfort to people, always showing that all power belongs to Allah.

📖 Truth with Courage

Some people believed in Isa, but others rejected him. Even so, he kept speaking the truth with patience and dignity.

Prophet Isa's story teaches us that gentleness and courage can go together. A believer can be kind and strong at the same time.

🌟 What We Learn from Isa's Story

✨

Allah can do what people think is impossible. The birth of Isa was a miracle from Allah.

🕊️

Speak truth with mercy. Isa called people with compassion and wisdom.

🤍

All miracles come from Allah. Prophets are honoured servants, and Allah is the source of power.

📖

Follow revelation. Prophets teach people how to worship Allah correctly.
